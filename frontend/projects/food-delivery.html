Food Delivery App

A food delivery mobile application that enables users to browse restaurants, order meals, and track deliveries through a clean and intuitive user experience.

2 Weeks

Project Overview

The Food Delivery App was designed to simplify the process of ordering food and tracking deliveries. The focus was on creating a user-friendly interface that allows users to quickly browse restaurants, place orders, and track their deliveries with ease.

Problem Statement

Many users struggle with complicated food ordering processes and lack easy access to restaurant information. This project aimed to create a seamless and accessible digital food delivery experience.

Key Learnings

  • Better information architecture.
  • Mobile-first thinking.
  • Component Consistency.

Final Outcome

The Food Delivery App successfully delivers a clean and intuitive experience for users to order food and browse restaurants. This project strengthened my UI/UX skills in designing accessible and user-centered food delivery solutions.
